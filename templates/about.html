About Brain Tumor Detection and Classification App

Welcome to the Brain Tumor Detection and Classification App. This application helps to detect and classify brain tumors using advanced deep learning techniques.

The app is designed to assist medical professionals in diagnosing brain tumors with greater accuracy and efficiency. By uploading MRI images, the app can quickly analyze and predict the type of tumor present.

  • Glioma Tumor
  • Meningioma Tumor
  • Pituitary Tumor
  • No Tumor

This tool is powered by a deep learning model trained on a large dataset of brain MRI images. The model uses ResNet-50 architecture, which is known for its accuracy in image classification tasks.
